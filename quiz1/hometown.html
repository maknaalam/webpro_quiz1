Surabaya

On my defends, I'm not memorizing all of the places because of how Surabaya is more of a busy city rather than a vacation destination. There aren't a lot of good quality tourist places to be remembered, mostly it's just malls that became the attraction of Surabaya. So that's why I intend to just not remembering the name of each places. But if you want my recommendation of tourist attractions, you can go to my tourist places page!
